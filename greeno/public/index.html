Welcome To Exquisite Florist

We fervently believe that flowers infuse every moment with vitality here. This belief is the result of a deep comprehension of the transformational potential of blooms. Flowers are the lovely representatives of nature that can bring so much life, color, and happiness into our lives. A celebration of life that transcends the commonplace and uplifts the spirit emerges with each petal and bloom. We nurture this idea in our sanctuary and incorporate it into each floral arrangement. Our dedication and enthusiasm are focused on spreading the captivating beauty of flowers, making sure that every interaction with our blooms enlivens the surroundings.

About Us

Welcome to Exquisite Florist, a blossoming sanctuary of beauty and passion, rooted in the vibrant community of Molapo, Soweto. Our journey began in 1993 when a visionary mother, fueled by her deep love for flowers and a desire for a meaningful second income, set the seeds of what would become a thriving florist business. With dedication and a heart full of petals, our founder, Mother Emelie, established a haven where the language of flowers was spoken fluently. Her commitment to quality, an eye for exquisite arrangements, and a genuine love for her craft have shaped Exquisite Florist into the cherished establishment it is today. Residing in the heart of Molapo, Soweto, Mother Emelie has become a local floral luminary, bringing joy and beauty to countless homes and events. Her journey in the floral industry has been a tapestry of experience, with each bloom telling a story of dedication, growth, and a passion that has bloomed into a legacy.
