About US

Welcome to Exquisite Florist, a blossoming sanctuary of beauty and passion, rooted in the vibrant community of Molapo, Soweto. Our journey began in 1993 when a visionary mother, fueled by her deep love for flowers and a desire for a meaningful second income, set the seeds of what would become a thriving florist business. With dedication and a heart full of petals, our founder, Mother Emelie, established a haven where the language of flowers was spoken fluently. Her commitment to quality, an eye for exquisite arrangements, and a genuine love for her craft have shaped Exquisite Florist into the cherished establishment it is today. Residing in the heart of Molapo, Soweto, Mother Emelie has become a local floral luminary, bringing joy and beauty to countless homes and events. Her journey in the floral industry has been a tapestry of experience, with each bloom telling a story of dedication, growth, and a passion that has bloomed into a legacy.

Our Funeral and Unveiling Flower Selection

Chrysanthemums and Roses bouquet

A breathtaking arrangement of chrysanthemums and roses creates a captivating bouquet that seamlessly blends elegance and vibrancy. The chrysanthemums, with their intricate petals and varied hues, add a touch of sophistication and symbolize joy and longevity. Intertwined with the timeless beauty of roses, which convey love and passion, this bouquet becomes a stunning ensemble of contrasting yet harmonious elements.

Transvaal-Daisy and Red Roses

In a poignant display of remembrance, a floral arrangement featuring all-yellow Transvaal daisies and deep red roses pays tribute to a life well-lived and the enduring memories left behind. The yellow Transvaal daisies, with their radiant and sunny disposition, symbolize the eternal nature of the soul, offering a sense of hope and optimism during a time of mourning. Intertwined with the solemn beauty of red roses, a symbol of love and respect, this bouquet becomes a poignant expression of both sorrow and celebration.

Dahlia all white and sunflowers

In a somber reflection of loss and the enduring spirit, an arrangement featuring all-white Dahlias and sunflowers offers a poignant tribute at a funeral. The purity and elegance of the all-white Dahlias symbolize a sense of reverence and grace, representing the departed's journey into the realm of peace and tranquility. Interwoven with the warmth of sunflowers, these blossoms bring a touch of sunshine to the mourning atmosphere, embodying adoration and loyalty.

Unveiling Setup with Roses

In the poignant moment of a tombstone unveiling, a striking display of blue flowers and red roses adorns the memorial site, creating a tapestry of emotions that intertwines love, remembrance, and reverence.

Heart Wreath

In the solemn embrace of grief, a funeral wreath, adorned with an array of serene blossoms, stands as a poignant tribute to a life now stilled. Composed with care and reverence, the wreath is a circular tapestry of blooms, symbolizing the everlasting cycle of life.

All White Coffin Spray

In the hushed reverence of a solemn farewell, an all-white coffin spray, meticulously arranged, adorns the final resting place with a serene elegance. each petal carries the weight of collective memories, a silent tribute to a life well-lived.
